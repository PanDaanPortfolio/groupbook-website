FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about getting started with GroupBook.

How quickly can I get started?

Most agents are sending their first quote requests within 30 minutes of signing up. We'll walk you through the setup during your demo.

Which hotels are in your database?

We have 200+ verified hotels including Southern Sun, Tsogo Sun, City Lodge, Protea by Marriott, Radisson, Sun International, and more.

Can I use my own branding?

Absolutely. All quote requests sent through GroupBook use your agency branding and contact details. Hotels respond directly to you.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your client data, quotes, and business information are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with competitors.

What if I need to add team members?

GroupBook supports team collaboration with proper access controls. Add colleagues and everyone works from the same system.

How long until hotels respond?

Response times vary by hotel, typically 24-48 hours. GroupBook tracks all responses in real-time so nothing falls through the cracks.
